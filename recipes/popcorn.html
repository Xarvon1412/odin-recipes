Popcorn

I hope you enjoy this very simple recipe for popcorn in a bowl. It's easy, greasy, and tastes great.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Popcorn Kernels
  • 4 Cups Butter (Melted)
  • 1 Bowl the size of your dreams

Directions

  1. Place the popcorn kernels in the bowl.
  2. Place the butter on top of the kernels and toss them in the butter.
  3. Place the bowl of kernels in the microwave.
  4. Set the mircowave for six minutes.
  5. Enjoy your popcorn and try to avoid any butter explosions coming from this bowl of goodness (and mild pain)!
