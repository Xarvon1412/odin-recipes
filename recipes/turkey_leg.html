Turkey Leg

This is a turkey leg. It's pretty good. It sounds to me like you're a little unaware of what a turkey leg is, but this is it.

Ingredients

  • 1 Turkey leg
  • Thyme
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Set oven to 350F
  2. Rub turkey leg with butter. Make sure to get under skin.
  3. Rub salt, pepper, and thyme onto the turkey leg.
  4. Put that thing in the oven for around twenty minutes.
  5. Take it out and eat my man.
